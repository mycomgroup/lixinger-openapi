# Assumptions Catalog (Default Ranges)

Use these ranges as starting points; adjust to market conditions.

## Discount Rate and Growth
- Risk-free rate: long-term local sovereign yield
- Market risk premium: 5% to 7% starting point
- Terminal growth: 1% to 3%
- Target capital structure: industry median

## Operating Assumptions
- Revenue growth: align with industry cycle and history
- Margin: anchor to 3 to 5 year average
- Capex: consistent with depreciation and expansion plan
- Working capital: track revenue, modest improvement

## Scenario Boundaries
- Upside: revenue +20% to +30%, margin +200 to +400 bps
- Downside: revenue -20% to -30%, margin -200 to -400 bps
- WACC shift: +/- 50 to 150 bps

## Industry Add-ons
- SaaS: NRR 100% to 130%, churn 5% to 15%
- Internet: ARPU growth 5% to 15%
- Resources: price deck aligned to futures curve
- Real estate: sell-through aligned to local inventory
