# Peer Selection Guidance

## Core Principles
- Match business model, margin structure, and revenue mix
- Align geography, currency, and accounting standards where possible
- Use similar scale or include size adjustments

## Filters
- Profitability and growth stage
- Leverage and capital intensity
- Regulatory regime
- Listing venue and liquidity

## Practical Steps
- Start with 8 to 15 comps
- Remove outliers after reviewing drivers
- Keep at least 5 comps in final set
- Document removals and rationale

## Output Expectations
- Final peer list with rationale
- Normalized multiples table
- Range and median summary
