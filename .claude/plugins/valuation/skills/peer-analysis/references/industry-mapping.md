# Industry Valuation Model Mapping

Use this mapping to select primary and secondary valuation models by industry type.

## Financials
- Banks: P/B + ROE, Residual Income, P/E
- Insurance: DDM, Embedded Value, P/EV, P/B
- Brokers: P/B + ROE, P/E, net capital constraints
- Asset managers: FCFF DCF, P/AUM, P/E
- Exchanges: FCFF DCF, P/E, EV/EBITDA

## Utilities and Infrastructure
- Regulated utilities: RAB-based DCF, EV/EBITDA, NAV
- Power generation: FCFF DCF, EV/EBITDA, EV/MW
- Telecom: FCFF DCF, EV/EBITDA, EV/Subscriber

## Industrials and Cyclicals
- Manufacturing: FCFF DCF, EV/EBITDA, ROIC
- Materials/chemicals: FCFF DCF, EV/EBITDA, P/E
- Shipping/airlines: FCFF DCF, EV/EBITDAR, NAV

## Resources
- Mining/oil & gas: NAV, rNPV, EV/Reserves, EV/EBITDA

## Consumer and Retail
- Staples: FCFF DCF, P/E, PEG
- Discretionary: FCFF DCF, P/E, P/S
- Offline retail: EV/EBITDA, EV/Store, NAV

## Internet and Software
- E-commerce: Scenario DCF, P/S, EV/GMV
- Platforms: Scenario DCF, P/User, EV/MAU
- SaaS: Scenario DCF, EV/ARR, P/S
- Semis/hardware: FCFF DCF, P/E, EV/EBITDA

## Healthcare
- Biotech: rNPV, options-based DCF, P/S
- Healthcare services: FCFF DCF, EV/EBITDA

## Autos
- OEMs: FCFF DCF, P/E, EV/EBITDA
- Dealers: FCFF DCF, P/E, EV/EBITDA
- EV makers: Scenario DCF, P/S

## Holdings and Conglomerates
- SOTP, EV/EBITDA, P/B
