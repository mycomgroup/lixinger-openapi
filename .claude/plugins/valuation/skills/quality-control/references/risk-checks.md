# Valuation Risk Checks

Use this checklist to identify common pitfalls and data anomalies in valuation work.

## 1. Earnings Quality
- Operating cash flow persistently below net income
- Receivables or inventory growing much faster than revenue
- One-off gains dominate reported profits

## 2. Capital Structure and Solvency
- Short-term debt concentration with refinancing risk
- Weak interest coverage
- Elevated impairment risk

## 3. Assumption Reasonableness
- Terminal value dominates enterprise value
- Terminal growth exceeds nominal GDP for long periods
- WACC/Ke inconsistent with industry risk

## 4. Relative Valuation Distortions
- Too few comps or biased peer set
- Inconsistent LTM vs NTM usage
- Large differences in business mix and margins

## 5. Industry-Specific Flags
- Financials: declining capital ratios or inadequate reserves
- Real estate: weak sell-through or liquidity pressure
- Internet: regulatory risk and rising traffic costs
- Resources: high commodity price volatility
