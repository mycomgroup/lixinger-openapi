# Valuation Data Requirements

This file lists the minimum inputs and consistency checks for multi-model valuation.

## 1. Core Financials (Required)
- Income statement: revenue, gross profit, EBIT, EBITDA, net income
- Balance sheet: cash, short/long debt, equity, minority interest
- Cash flow: operating cash flow, capex, working capital changes
- Use LTM or latest annual report; keep TTM and NTM consistent

## 2. Market and Capital Structure (Required)
- Market cap, shares outstanding, share price
- Risk-free rate, market risk premium, beta
- Cost of debt, credit spread, tax rate
- Net debt, preferreds, minority interest
- FX exposure and inflation assumptions

## 3. Macro and Industry (Recommended)
- Industry cycle indicators, supply/demand data
- Regulatory and tax policy changes
- Penetration, concentration, competitive landscape

## 4. Operating Metrics (As Applicable)
- Users and traffic: MAU, DAU, retention, ARPU, conversion
- Subscription: ARR, NRR, GRR, churn
- Capacity: design capacity, utilization, unit capex
- Unit economics: store-level or ticket-level economics
- Reserves: mining/oil & gas reserves and grades
- Orders and pipeline: backlog, R&D milestones
- Platform: GMV, TPV, take rate

## 5. Financial Institutions
- Asset quality: NPL, coverage, migration
- Capital: CET1, capital adequacy, leverage
- Margin/expense: NIM, cost-to-income
- Insurance: EV, NBV, VIF, premium mix
- Asset management: AUM, fee rate, retention

## 6. Real Estate and REITs
- Land bank, project progress, sell-through
- FFO, AFFO, rent growth, vacancy
- Asset type and lease terms

## 7. Tech and Internet
- User growth, acquisition cost, retention curves
- LTV/CAC, ARPU/ASP, gross margin
- Compute cost, server capex

## 8. Energy and Commodities
- Reserves, grades, extraction cost
- Expansion plan and long-term price deck
- Carbon cost and policy changes

## 9. Transaction and Comps
- Trading multiples (LTM/NTM)
- Precedent transactions and control premium
- Key terms and earn-outs

## 10. Consistency Checks
- LTM vs NTM, parent vs consolidated, adjusted vs reported
- Remove one-offs: restructuring, asset sales, subsidies
- Reconcile cash flow vs earnings quality
- Review debt maturity profile and refinancing risk
- Validate capex vs depreciation and growth plans

## 11. Derived Metrics
- ROE, ROIC, NOPAT, EBITDA margin
- WACC, Ke, Kd
- Unit economics (LTV/CAC, ARPU/ASP, gross margin)
- Scenario drivers (growth, margin, capex)
